## Files

- `Mahdi (Matt) Ghadimi_Week 4 Take-Home Assignment_Fall 2025 Data Science Bootcamp.ipynb`  
  - Contains all solutions for Week 4 homework problems, including **both SQL and Pandas implementations**:  
    - **Actors and Directors Who Cooperated At Least Three Times**  
      - Identify all actor-director pairs with at least three collaborations.  
    - **Fix Names in a Table**  
      - Standardize names so that only the first character is uppercase.  
    - **Combine Two Tables**  
      - Merge `Person` and `Address` tables with proper handling of missing addresses.  
    - **Second Highest Salary**  
      - Compute the second highest distinct salary; handle cases with no second highest.  
    - **List the Products Ordered in a Period**  
      - Filter orders in February 2020 with at least 100 units and summarize total units.  
    - **Replace Employee ID with the Unique Identifier**  
      - Map employees to their unique IDs; show null where missing.  
    - **Game Play Analysis IV**  
      - Compute fraction of players logging in the day after their first login.  
    - **Project Employees I**  
      - Compute average experience years for employees on each project, rounded to two decimals.  
    - **Department Top Three Salaries**  
      - Identify employees with top three unique salaries in each department.
